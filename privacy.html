2. 제3자 서비스

게임은 아래 서비스를 사용하며, 각 서비스는 자체 개인정보처리방침에 따라 정보를 처리합니다.

  • Supabase — 게임 데이터 서버 저장 (방침)
  • Unity LevelPlay / ironSource — 광고 제공 (방침)
  • Unity Analytics — 게임 플레이 분석 (방침)
  • Apple App Store — 결제 처리 (방침)

3. 보관 및 파기

수집된 정보는 서비스 제공 기간 동안 보관됩니다. 게임 내 설정 → 계정 설정 → 계정 삭제를 통해 언제든 계정과 모든 데이터를 영구 삭제할 수 있으며, 삭제 시 서버에서 즉시 파기됩니다.

4. 아동의 개인정보

게임은 만 13세 미만 아동을 대상으로 하지 않으며, 아동의 개인정보를 고의로 수집하지 않습니다.

5. 이용자의 권리

이용자는 자신의 정보에 대한 열람·정정·삭제를 요청할 수 있습니다. 광고 식별자 수집은 iOS 설정(설정 → 개인정보 보호 → 추적)에서 언제든 철회할 수 있습니다.

Privacy Policy (English)

Why Planet ("we") operates the mobile game Sky Bounce. This policy explains what information the game collects and how it is used. Effective date: July 3, 2026.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers — an anonymous, auto-generated account ID (UUID) and your nickname, used for save data, leaderboards, and account recovery.
  • Social sign-in data — when you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive the account identifier and email (if you choose to share it) to link your progress across devices.
  • Game data — scores, progress, virtual currency, skins, achievements, and settings, stored on our servers for cloud save.
  • Device information — device model, OS version, language, and app version, for compatibility and error analysis.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) — collected only if you allow tracking via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt; used for ad delivery, frequency capping, and fraud prevention.
  • Purchase receipts — issued by Apple, used solely to verify purchases and grant items. We never access your payment card details.

2. Third-Party Services

The game uses Supabase (server hosting), Unity LevelPlay/ironSource (advertising), Unity Analytics (gameplay analytics), and Apple App Store (payments). Each processes data under its own privacy policy, linked above.

3. Retention & Deletion

Data is retained while you use the service. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time in-game via Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deletion takes effect immediately on our servers.

4. Children

The game is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. You can withdraw tracking consent at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

6. Contact

For privacy inquiries: whyplanet.dev@gmail.com
